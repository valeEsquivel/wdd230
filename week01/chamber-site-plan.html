Site Purpose

1. The Chamber of Commerce of Sonsonate acts as an entity that permanently promotes and defends the system of free initiative, promoting the unity of our members, and business development with social responsibility, leading actions and facilitating services that promote competitiveness and innovation of our members, protecting their rights.

2. The Chamber of Commerce of Sonsonate contributes to the community by increasing the competitiveness of companies, promoting trade and investment, generating the best opportunities for development with social responsibility, while generating jobs through the growth of our associates.
